About High-Rise Airline

High-Rise Airline was created to provide a simple, reliable, and efficient air travel experience within the Philippines. It focuses on solving common travel issues by using a modern digital system that allows easy booking, reservation management, and real-time updates.

The airline aims to make domestic travel more accessible and convenient for Filipinos by offering affordable flights, comfortable services, and dependable schedules. Passengers choose High-Rise Airline for its user-friendly system, quality service, and smooth travel experience.

MISSION

To provide safe, affordable, and efficient domestic air travel across the Philippines through innovative systems and excellent customer service.

VISION

To become a leading domestic airline in the Philippines known for reliability, comfort, and customer satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a flight? +
You can book directly through our website by selecting your destination, travel dates, and preferred flight, then completing the payment process.
Can I hold a reservation without paying immediately? +
Some fares allow temporary holds. Availability depends on the route and fare type.
How do I know if my booking is confirmed? +
You will receive a confirmation email with your booking reference after payment is completed.
Can I book for someone else? +
Yes, you can enter passenger details for another person during booking.

Payments

What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept major credit/debit cards, online banking, and select e-wallets.
Is it safe to pay online? +
Yes, our system uses secure encryption to protect your payment information.
Why was my payment declined? +
This may be due to bank restrictions, insufficient funds, or incorrect details. Please contact your bank or try another method.

Baggage

What is the baggage allowance? +
Baggage allowance depends on your ticket type and route. Details are shown during booking.
Can I purchase extra baggage? +
Yes, you can add extra baggage during booking or via the "Manage Booking" section.
What items are not allowed in baggage? +
Dangerous goods such as flammable items, explosives, and certain batteries are prohibited.

Check-in

When can I check in online? +
Online check-in usually opens 24â€“48 hours before departure.
Do I need to print my boarding pass? +
You may print it or use a mobile boarding pass, depending on airport requirements.
What happens if I miss check-in? +
You may not be allowed to board, and your ticket could be forfeited depending on fare rules.

Changes & Cancellations

Can I change my flight after booking? +
Yes, changes are allowed depending on your fare type. Fees may apply.
How do I cancel my booking? +
You can cancel through the "Manage Booking" section or contact support.
Will I get a refund if I cancel? +
Refund eligibility depends on your ticket type and cancellation timing.

Account & Login

Do I need an account to book a flight? +
No, but creating an account allows easier tracking and faster future bookings.
I forgot my password. What should I do? +
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset it.

Special Services

Can I request special assistance? +
Yes, passengers requiring assistance can request it during booking or by contacting support.
Can I travel with pets? +
Pet travel policies vary. Please check our guidelines or contact support before booking.

General Information
